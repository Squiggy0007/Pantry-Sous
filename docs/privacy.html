Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 4, 2026

Pantry Sous is designed to help you manage household food inventory, discover recipes, plan meals, and create shopping lists. This Privacy Policy explains how Pantry Sous handles information.

Information Stored on Your Device

Pantry Sous stores app data locally on your device, including pantry ingredients, shopping list items, saved recipes, custom recipes, meal plans, barcode memory, and related nutrition details.

This information is used to provide app features such as recipe matching, shopping list creation, meal planning, and ingredient organization.

No Account Required

Pantry Sous does not require you to create an account. The app does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or login credentials.

Camera Access

Pantry Sous may request camera access so you can scan product barcodes. Camera access is used only for barcode scanning inside the app. Pantry Sous does not record video or store camera images.

Third-Party Services

Pantry Sous uses third-party services to provide recipe, barcode, product, and nutrition information. When you use related features, the app may send search terms, ingredient names, product names, or barcode numbers to these services so they can return relevant results.

  • Spoonacular may be used for recipe search and recipe details.
  • Open Food Facts may be used for barcode and product lookup.
  • USDA FoodData Central may be used for nutrition lookup.

These requests are used to provide app functionality. Pantry Sous does not sell your personal information. Third-party services may process requests according to their own privacy policies.

Advertising and Tracking

Pantry Sous does not currently include advertising and does not track you across apps or websites.

Analytics

Pantry Sous does not currently include third-party analytics SDKs.

Data Sharing

Pantry Sous does not sell your data. Pantry Sous does not share your locally stored pantry, recipe, shopping list, or meal planning data with the developer unless you choose to send information through a support email or another communication channel.

Deleting Your Data

Because Pantry Sous stores app data locally on your device, you can delete app data by deleting the app from your device. You can also remove individual ingredients, shopping list items, saved recipes, and custom recipes inside the app.

Children's Privacy

Pantry Sous is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will be updated when changes are made.
